Carbon Runner

About the Game

Carbon Runner is a fast-paced endless runner where players dodge polluting obstacles and collect green power-ups. It encourages eco-awareness and sustainable travel through gamified mechanics.

Eco-Friendly Features

  • Emissions meter to show how transport choices impact the planet.
  • Points earned for riding bikes, using electric buses, or planting trees.
  • Low power mode optimized for energy efficiency on mobile devices.
