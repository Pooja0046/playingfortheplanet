Seedlings

About the Game

Seedlings is a cozy ecosystem strategy game. Players plant native trees and nurture landscapes over time, with growth patterns influenced by actual climate data. Designed to promote biodiversity awareness.

Eco-Friendly Features

  • Uses real reforestation data to influence in-game ecosystems.
  • Includes in-game tips on biodiversity and sustainable planting.
  • Energy-efficient low-res textures.

Quick Info

  • Genre: Strategy / Simulation
  • Built With: Unity
  • Platform: Desktop
  • Team: Green Sprout Studio
