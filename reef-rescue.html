Reef Rescue

About the Game

Reef Rescue is a strategy simulation where players rebuild coral reefs, combat invasive species, and balance biodiversity. Educational prompts guide users through real-world ocean conservation methods.

Eco-Friendly Features

  • Live reef maps based on real restoration sites.
  • In-game partnerships with marine NGOs for education and outreach.
  • Minimalist visual design for low energy consumption.
